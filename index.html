⚔️ Your Mission

Navigate through 5 chambers, each testing your knowledge of pedagogical frameworks. Answer ALL questions correctly in each chamber to unlock the next!

❤️ Life Powers

Each warrior has 2 Life Powers. Your team starts with up to 8 total lives. Wrong answers at the passcode gate cost 1 life. If all lives are lost, your quest ends!

🛡️ Chamber Aids (3 per level)

  • 🎯 50/50 — Removes 2 wrong answers
  • 📖 Framework Reference — View framework details
  • 💡 Hint — Get a guiding clue for the level

🔑 Passcode Gates

After answering 5 questions, enter the passcode from your facilitator. ALL 5 answers must be correct to pass!

🏆 Victory

Complete all 5 chambers to escape the fortress and earn your Framework Master certificate!
